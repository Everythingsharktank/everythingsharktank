## QUICK TIPS FOR CAPCUT
1. Import the clip
2. Add text → choose bold white font with black stroke
3. Set each text block to show for 3-5 seconds
4. Add a trending sound at 15% volume (browse trending in CapCut)
5. Export at 1080x1920 (9:16)
6. Upload to TikTok — add auto-captions when posting

**Time per video: ~10-15 minutes in CapCut once you get the hang of it**
